Anime Conventions & Casino

Anime conventions include contests. Contests can focus around costumes (or cosplay), art works (drawn, sculpted, painted), anime-related music videos, video games, dice games, card games, and many other activities. In some cases, tangible prizes have been offered as winnings in these contests. An Exhibit Hall or Dealers' Room is also popular at anime conventions. Publishing companies, distributors, and other proprietors often arrive to exhibit and/or sell their newest products to fans.

RPG & PVP Games

Anime RPGs and/or JRPGs have been on everyones watchlist for decades, more so now due to the quarantine. People have been digging up old titles and are on a constant lookout on steam for possible rereleases of old games with a PC patch or new titles. Theres just something about the unique battle systems, game mechanics, character building, anime-like graphics and a lot of other features that make Anime RPGs/JRPGs so much more engaging and enchanting than a regular RPG.
